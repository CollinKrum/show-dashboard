Starter Help

Guides

Simple starting points for using the database, finding flips, and working through collections without bouncing between mixed layouts.
Players
Find the Right Cards Fast
Use the Players page to filter by team, position, and series. Start by sorting by overall, then switch to power, contact, speed, or fielding depending on what kind of lineup you want.
Open Players
Market
Check the Best Flips
Use the Market page to look for profitable cards first. Focus on cards with healthy profit after tax and enough spread between buy and sell so minor price movement does not kill the flip.
Open Market
Collections
Track Missing Cards
Use Collections to mark owned cards and quickly see what is still missing by series. The cheapest missing view is the easiest way to grind toward completion without overspending.
Open Collections

Best way to use the site

1. Start on Players and search for the card or team you care about.
2. Jump to Market to see where the flip opportunities are strongest.
3. Use Collections when you want to turn those stubs into set progress.
4. Keep an eye on series names and rarities so you can spot where value piles up.
